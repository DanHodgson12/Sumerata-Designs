About Us

We are Janet and Pauline, crafting sisters from Somerset.

We grew up on the Somerset levels and Pauline still lives there, and this is reflected in some of our work.

We have tried out many crafts over the years and are currently concentrating on selling lino prints and handmade cards, precious metal clay jewellery (PMC) and sewn & crocheted gifts. All of our items are original designs made by us.
