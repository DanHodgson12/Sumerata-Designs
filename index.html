Who We Are

We are Janet and Pauline, crafting sisters from Somerset. We grew up on the Somerset levels and Pauline still lives there, which is reflected in some of our work.

We've tried out many crafts over the years and are currently concentrating on selling lino prints and handmade cards, precious metal clay jewellery (PMC) and crocheted gifts. All of our items are original designs made by us.
